AGENT ROLES

As a new Tactical First-Person Shooter game with an emphasis on abilities, VALORANT players use a unique mix of terminology to match the roles found within the game. In this guide we go over those roles and how they function within the game.

Duelist

Self-sufficient fraggers who their team expects, through abilities and skills, to get high frags and seek out engagements first.

Initiator

Challenge angles by setting up their team to enter contested ground and push defenders away.

Controller

Experts in slicing up dangerous territory to set their team up for success.

Sentinel

Defensive experts who can lock down areas and watch flanks, both on attacker and defender rounds.
